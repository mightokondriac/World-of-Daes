====== Ordrinn ======
@odem @origins @worlddata @article

Metal: Eilon
Effect/Affinity: Star
	At the same moment his brother, Byalinn, had laid witness to their sister Muwinn cast her bright eyes on the world and bring love of light to the golden metal of the world; Ordrinn looked to the moon as well. Beyond the orb of silvery light, Ordrinn looked to the stars; a sea of wonder and enchantment and it was his love to look upon them --as ever they shined ever in the darkness, revealing places far distant and unimagined.
