++ Nevrenis
[alias: The Nevers]

Metal: Unknown
Effect/Affinity: Never Effect
Neverenis was sent to guard the minds of the new metals brought about by Meark, and all of their creations, to ensure the protection and abidance of the One Law; "Love this World, and with that love, bring love into the minds of those you harbor".
