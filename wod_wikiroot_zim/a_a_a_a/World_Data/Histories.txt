====== Histories ======
Created Wednesday 05 January 2022

The historical events that shaped the world. These are generally framed as an understanding of how things are by a specific race or races. These histories are more documentary than fantastical story. Not all of this information is available to a reader or even the characters involved in the world. These histories are the actual thing that happened.
