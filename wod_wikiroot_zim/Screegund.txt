====== Screegund ======
@lordoftrivault @screegund @falseodem @node @worlddata

The Scorpion Lord(or crab lord) - One of the Lords of Trivault
creator of the divergent wing, bringer of the [[Salt Smiter]].
the crab god that manifests through a trickery in the way [[Meark]] introduced new elements expressed as the [[Mirodem]]

Screegund was born and died in the bosom of the Great Wing Mountains. The Northern Wing Mountains are said to house the location of Screegund_s burial mound.
