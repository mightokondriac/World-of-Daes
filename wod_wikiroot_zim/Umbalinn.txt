++ Umbalinn

Metal: Ertin
Effect/Affinity: Stone
	Sister of Ryyndal of the Tesdema. Together they saw the majesty of the heaving and hardening of the earth of stone and fire. Umbalinn could see and feel the power of the stone and it was her love; the stuff of permanency but that is proof that all things do in time change.
