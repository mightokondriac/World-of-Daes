====== Fornaas Listuary ======
Created Friday 07 January 2022

One of the two Great Libraries that held chronicles and artifacts from all races of the landmass knowns as Godorand(Primary Continent of the stories within the scope of this project).

[[Kuptra Fiaine]] visits the Fornaas Listuary to write his book A Treatise on Tea, one of his most inegmatic works ever written.
