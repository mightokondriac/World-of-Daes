====== Godorand ======
@chrondata @worlddata @node @chronicle @3rdage @godorand @landmass

The primary continent of Daes.

Established as the colonies of Sekor, Godor, Medrit and Yeshimin in the 3rd age of the common recolection. Over the next age the landscape changed drastically from large unmarked swathes of land, all functioning together as groups with understanding, to smaller tribes and races clammoring for power.  In the wake of the Patterns of the Odem being disrupted by treachery of [[Screegund]] the False Lord of Trivault.
